Overview

- Jaypee Institute of Information Technology
- JIIT Noida was established in the year 2001
- A 10, A Block, Block A, Industrial Area, Sector 62, Noida, Uttar Pradesh 201309
- Area: 15.5 acres (JIIT Noida)
- Jaypee has 4 Colleges : 1.(JIIT), Noida, U.P 2. (JUIT), Solan, H.P 3. (JUET), Guna, M.P, 4. (JUA), Bulandshahr, U.P
- JIIT 2022 NIRF Engineering Rankings - 94


Location

- A 10, A Block, Block A, Industrial Area, Sector 62, Noida, Uttar Pradesh 201309


Admission Process

- One needs to take the JEE Main exam - JIIT, JUET, JUIT
- Candidate should have minimum of 60% marks (55% for SC/ST) in aggregate in 10+2 or its equivalent examination.


Fees

Year wise Academic fee for B.Tech Programme:

- First year : 310,200
- Second year : 325,700
- Third year : 341,900
-Forth year : 359,000


Courses

- JIIT offers following branches for B.Tech Programmes at the Undergraduate Level:
- Electronics and Communication Engineering
- Computer Science and Engineering
- Information Technology
- Biotechnology


Cut-Off


2021-22 Round-3, Delhi General
