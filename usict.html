USICT Review

University School of Information Communication and Technology

University School of Information, Communication and Technology is a constituent institute of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University accredited grade 'A++' by NAAC located in university's main campus at Dwarka Sec-16, Dwarka, Delhi.

Overview

- Affiliated to : Guru Gobind Singh Indraprasth University
- Full name : University School of Information, Communication and Technology
- Established in the year 1999
- Located in New Delhi
- Institute : Government
- Campus Area : 60 Acres
- NIRF Engineering Ranking 101 - 150


Location

- Dwarka Sector-16 C, Dwarka, Delhi, 110078
- Covered with many malls around it like Vegas Mall and many other


Admission Process

- One needs to take the JEE Main exam.
- One also need to score at least 55% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in class XII provided the candidate has passed in each subject separately. Candidate must additionally have to passed English as a subject of study in the qualifying examination.
- Admission is done on the basis of rank in JEE Mains through GGSIPU Counslling.


Fees

Year wise Academic fee for B.Tech Programme:

- First year : 132,000
- Second year Onwards: 120,000


Courses

- NSUT offers following branches for B.Tech Programmes at the Undergraduate Level:
1. Computer Science and Engineering (60 seats)
2. Electronics and Communication Engineering (60 seats)
3. Information Technology (60 seats)
Total seats are 180

Facilities

- A bank and ATM have been provided on campus for the convenience of staff and students.
- Hostel: Hostel facility is available separately for both boys and girls. Food is very nice.
- Library with WiFi
- Auditorium: Different auditorium for different blocks.
- Sports: Institute offers various facilities and equipment to its students. Campus has Basket ball court, Cricket ground, Volleyball court, Badminton court, Swimming pool, Table tennis etc.
- Campus also has GYM, which is only for Hostel students.
- Canteen, Nescafe, Amul, Mother dairy and different food carts.
