MAIT Review

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology

Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology is established by Maharaja Agrasen Technical Education Society promoted by a group of well known Industrialists, Businessman, Professionals and Philanthropists with an aim to promote quality education in the field of Technology.
MAIT endeavors to provide industry relevant education and training to its well-crafted practical training programs for the student in different semesters of the course.

Overview

- Affiliated to: Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University
- Full name: Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology
- Established in the year 1999
- Located in New Delhi
- Institute : Private
- Campus Area : 40.5 Acres
- NIRF Engineering Ranking 201-250


Location

- Plot No 1 Rohini, Plot No 1, CH Bhim Singh Nambardar Marg, Sector 22, PSP Area, Delhi, 110086


Admission Process

- JEE Mains
- Counselling - GGSIPU Counselling
- MAIT Delhi Admission- UG The eligibility for admission to MAIT Delhi B. Tech programmes is that the candidates' must have passed the 10+2 exam and secured an aggregate of 45% marks (40% marks in case of candidates belonging to the reserved category).


Fees

Year wise Academic fee for B.Tech Programme:

- First year : 143,000
- Second year Onwards: 130,000


Courses

- MAIT offers following branches for B.Tech Programmes at the Undergraduate Level:
1. Computer Science and Engineering (180 seats-1st shift + 60 seats-2nd shift)
2. Electronics and Communication Engineering (180 seats)
3. Electrical and Electronics Engineering (180 seats)
4. Information Technology (180 seats)
5. Mechanical and Automation Engineering (60 seats)
6. Mechanical Engineering (60 seats)
7. Computer Science & Technology (60 seats)
8. Information Technology and Engineering (60 seats)
9. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML) (60 seats)
10. Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI&DS) (60 seats)


Cut-Off


2021-22 Round-3, Delhi General



Placements


USICT 2022 Placement Stats:

- Highest Package : 57 LPA
- Average Package : 12 LPA


Facilities

- Hostel: Hostel facility is available separately for both boys and girls. Food is very nice.
- Library
- Auditorium: The institute is equipped with an auditorium.
- Sports: Institute offers various facilities and equipment to its students. Campus has Basket ball court, Cricket ground, Volleyball court etc.
- Campus also has GYM, which is for all the students.
- Canteen, Nescafe, Amul and different food carts.
